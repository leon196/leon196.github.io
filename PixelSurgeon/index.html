Pixel Surgeon Prototype

Pixel Surgeon is inspirated by Rick Levine's Microsurgeon and Conway's Game of Life.

You are inside a human body ravaged by sickness.
Sickness is ruled by a cellular automata
Destroy sick cells with your lazer
Reconstruct the vital cells

Controls :
Move with arrows
Lazer with clic
Aim with mouse

There is not yet real win and lost condition.
Game still need to be improved.
