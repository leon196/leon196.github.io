Digital swamp scrolling (2023)

A project made for Sickhouse festival Overkill of 2023. The "The Outburst of the Digital Swamp" theme inspired us the concept of scrolling through a rotting website, where code source of pages would slowly melt and be decomposed into floating components. While digital tadpoles would swim between images and texts, commenting and reacting with emojis to the content, like a wild natural social network.

Made with Emilie Breslavetz.
