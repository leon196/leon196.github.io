Crash Party

buggy playful exhibition

project, exhibition

2023

Léon Denise, Emilie Breslavetz, Elie Gavoty

In 2023, the Cookie Collective were invited for live coding performances at A MAZE. festival in Berlin. The place had an extra room for an exhibition, so we had the opportunity to make something special. With Emilie Breslavetz, we created a playful and experimental exhibition: the Crash Party. People could play games with alternative controllers and be immerged in a world of errors and bugs. This is where we created Crashboard, our cybersurfing game played with a skateboard.

Les Espaces Rêvés

live digital performance

Léon Denise, Dorian Rigal, HFLOW

project, vr, kinect, pointcloud

2021

Les Espaces Rêvés is a performative short film. It is part of the Hétérotopia series, which explores the spaces that can connect us to our utopias. The idea is to open up the boundary between the work and the viewer by inviting them to enter the screen.

The work pays homage to all the urbanities that make up the Paris metropolis. The aim is to create a shared imaginary of the area by directly using the materials that make it up. The work takes the form of tableaux created using 3D scans and a volumetric camera. Neon Minuit depicts bits of cities, architecture and means of transport. The buildings and bodies scanned are represented as a cloud of coloured dots on the screen. These dots are animated to create effects of movement, paint and texture.
