Orbital

spherical bullet hell cybercrime

Orbital is a spherical bullet hell on rail controlled by a real globe.

You play as a cyber criminal who hacked the security of a military satellite. You exploit this planetary device to harvest sensible data and sell it to the dark web. One day you receive a strange message about an agency recruiting talent like you. Will you accept to engage deeper into serious cyber criminality?
