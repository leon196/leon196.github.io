Crashboard

cybersurfing experience

Crashboard is a surfing cyberspace game controlled by a skateboard.

It is the first game of my collaboration with Emilie Breslavetz which emerge from the first Crash Party in 2023. From it's first prototype to it's final form today, Crashboard is a tribute to the early imagination of cyberspace culture.
