Heterotopia

Virtual reality experience (2020)

with Dorian Rigal (Neon Minuit)

The first prototype of a virtual reality experience with animated volumetric capture recorded and live. It serves as a technical demo for Heterotopia Miroir et Perspective. We explored many experimentals concepts. One the main experience was to see his body made of point cloud, inside a virtual reality headset. We would be able to track body and hands with the Kinect camera, enabling interactions between our real body and the virtual world.
