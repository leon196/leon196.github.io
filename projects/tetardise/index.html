Têtardise

Têtardise is an internet roaming experience, using the wheel of a coffee mill to scroll the history of a web navigator. While you're diving through websites in decomposition, a social network herd simulation will crawl in between digital data. Use a heart shaped button to make the individuals react to online content and find out the narrative in between the browser history.
