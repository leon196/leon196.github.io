Crash Party 2023

Panke Culture, Berlin, Germany. May 11, 2023

The Cookie Collective were invited by AMaze, the playful media art festival in Berlin, to showcase live coding audio visual performances. There was an opportunity to use a gallery space to present installations, so with Emilie Breslavetz we elaborated an exhibition about crash, error and bug.

The Crash Party was born.
