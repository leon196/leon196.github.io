Cookie Party 2019

Landy Sauvage, Saint-Denis, France. October 26, 2019

With the members of the Cookie Collective, we organized a digital art event that showcases talks, workshops, performances, playful installations, experimental video games, video mapping and live coding in one place. It was a new format of our annual Cookie Collective demoparty event, that will include not only the demoscene culture but celebrates all form of digital art creation.

I organized a bring your own beamer for the event, calling for participation in many communities and among friends. Indie video game developpers, glitch artists, motion designers and video mapping artists. With the help of Dorian Rigal and the artists themselves, we build towers of projectors, with a total of 16 beamers running during the evening.
