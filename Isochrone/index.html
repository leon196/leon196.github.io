Isochrone,
a cooperative synchronization game for two players

Synchronize the keys race to progress
Player 1: Press S & F (speed up the external paths keys)
Player 2: Press J & L (speed down the internal paths keys)

A game by Mechanical Birdie
Design & Development: Léon Denise, Tatiana Vilela
