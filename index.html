Light Path
2016
The project was inspired by light painting. The intention was to capture a visual marker, held by an artist like a painter or a dancer, and interpret it by visuals and sound generation.
The project can detect 4 colors, tracking the center and the density of a color mass.
Each color has a music, and the volume of each music is synchronised with the density of the color.
It was made during the Culture Experience Days : Creative Weekend by ADAMI
With : Raphaelle, Alice and Julien

2016
A game about motion and movement, pixel and effects.
The original intention was to play the game with the trajectory of a ping pong ball. You had to play the game with a game of ping pong. Now you play with your body or whatever you want.
