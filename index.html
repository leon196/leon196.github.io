Heterotopia, Miroirs et Perspectives

Virtual reality experience

Neon Minuit

2020

A multiplayer, participatory virtual reality experience that immerses your body in a field of point clouds. The user is equipped with a VR headset and explores within a designated physical area where viewers can enter and create interactions within the VR space. A Kinect is placed above the play area and records people, elements and the environment. Finally, a video projector projects the virtual space into the real world on a screen visible to the audience.

Made with Dorian Rigal, Samuel Lecoeur and Rémi Large

Miroir Arcade

Playful installation

Incontrolab

2022

Arcade Mirror is an interactive multiplayer projection controlled by a cluster of handcrafted gamepads. The visitor sees their body projected into the digital space through a Kinect camera. Mysterious hanging boxes in space can generate spectacular effects. By sharing the controls, the participants manipulate buttons, potentiometers and switches to discover all the distorting filters.

Made with Dorian Rigal and Emilie Breslavetz

Mapping Inversé

Digital sculpture

Neon Minuit

2022

The sculpture is composed of a screen placed behind the transparent resin print of a Quadro 4000 graphics card. The liquid crystals are then hidden behind the technological artifact until the backlight of the screen turns on and reveals each pixel. Floating on the right of the screen, a computer calculates generative animations in real time. In the manner of architectural mapping, the visuals play with the shapes of the graphics card and create the illusion of a collision.

Made with Dorian Rigal

Calculateur Particulier

Digital sculpture

Neon Minuit

2022

Reflecting a computer tower, the screen facade opens like a portal, inviting the viewer to approach the heart of the machine. The screen and the central processing unit become one. Made with Dorian Rigal

Attrape Rêve

L'Attrape Rêve is a performance and a participatory digital installation

Neon Minuit

2019

A video and audio work in which the audience is invited to explore the world of dreams. The work is performed by 3 artists. The first is a shaman who invites members of the audience to lie down on a dream table, which allows the body to be scanned and the dream images to be extracted. The second is an illusionist who is responsible for operating the dream channels captured by the scanner. He manages the frequencies emitted by the lying body and transcribes them on the screen, the dome. The third is a musician who creates the narration and accompanies the audience with a sound ambiance and spoken texts that guide people on their journey.

Made with Dorian Rigal

Heterotopia

Virtual reality experience

Neon Minuit

2020

The first prototype of a virtual reality experience with animated volumetric capture recorded and live. It serves as a technical demo for Heterotopia Miroir et Perspective. We explored many experimentals concepts. One the main experience was to see his body made of point cloud, inside a virtual reality headset. We would be able to track body and hands with the Kinect camera, enabling interactions between our real body and the virtual world.

Made with Dorian Rigal
