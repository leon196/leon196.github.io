About
I am a urban citizen dreaming about coding in the countryside, hugging trees and talking to cows.
I like illustration, comics, games, interactive experiences and procedural visualisation.
I love starting new projects but finishing them is another story. Code is hard, pixel is tough and time is rare.

Feel free to contact me for any question or request. I would be pleased to share my work with you.
